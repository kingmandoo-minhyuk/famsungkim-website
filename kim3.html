Introducing

MINHYUK (a.k.a KIM3)

Perhaps the saying 'like attracts like' holds true? The energetic SUNG3 brought KIM3 – a man just like her, or perhaps possessing even more sparkling charm – home to meet SUNG1 and KIM1.

With his humorous, affectionate, and bright personality, KIM3, alongside his wife SUNG3, is like one half of a dynamic duo responsible for livening up the atmosphere of this quiet(?) family. Furthermore, he acts as the family's Creative Director, taking charge of all creative activities. He oversees everything from editing the family YouTube channel videos and creating the website to producing and managing all media content related to this world trip, such as photos and videos.

He comes from a background as an artist who serialized popular digital comics called 'webtoons' in Korea. Having successfully completed his series, he now pours all his passion into documenting and sharing the family's precious world trip experiences through engaging media content. (If you want to see his work, click the logo at the top of the site)

However, there's an interesting twist to the bright and sociable KIM3: he claims to be, in fact, 'more introverted than anyone'! According to him, he developed his current extroverted demeanor through conscious effort, but perhaps only KIM3 knows the full story. Perhaps when you encountered this family, KIM3 was the one who approached you first. He genuinely loves listening to people's stories. (Though he carries the small secret that doing so drains his energy quite quickly!)
