Introducing

JUNGWON (a.k.a KIM2)

If you meet KIM2 for the first time, it might take quite a while before you hear him speak. That's because he's usually quite taciturn and quiet. However, once he starts speaking, you'll find yourself listening intently to the deep inner thoughts hidden beneath his quietness, and soon you'll want to hear more of what he has to say.

KIM2 is very intellectual and knowledgeable – truly a 'brainy guy' ('뇌섹남' as we say in Korean, meaning someone whose intelligence is charming). He possesses a brilliant mind, having graduated from Seoul National University, widely regarded as Korea's top university. One of his amazing abilities is languages; besides Korean, of course, he fluently speaks English, Spanish, Chinese, and Japanese – a total of 5 languages, making him like a 'human translator'!

He usually enjoys quietly listening to music or reading books in English during his downtime, but recently he has also shown an unexpected side, getting engrossed in watching YouTube and playing games. Fittingly for a music lover, on this family world trip, he's also capably fulfilling the role of music director, responsible for the playlists that enrich our journey.

This was KIM2, the charming eldest son-in-law, hiding deep intellect, versatility, and warmth beneath his quiet exterior.
