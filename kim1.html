Introducing

JAEYOUN (a.k.a KIM1)

Our family's treasure, KIM1, who is like a 'warm-hearted, spunky girl'! While she usually appears calm and meticulous, hidden inside is a cheerful, youthful spirit that loves fun more than anyone and is full of humor.

KIM1 is the ultimate homemaker, diligently ensuring all six family members are well-fed with delicious, lovingly prepared meals every day. Furthermore, she is the strongest supporter for all of us, always listening attentively to her daughters and sons-in-law and offering them unwavering support.

One of KIM1's hidden talents is her amazing sewing skill; she's so adept that if she decides to, she can whip up a beautiful Hanbok (traditional Korean dress) all by herself! Reflecting her love for flowers and plants, she is a naturally gifted creator ('golden hands', as we say in Korean) who uses her delicate touch to craft unique, beautiful bags and charming accessories from fabric.

Possessing a warm heart, youthful cheerfulness, incredible dexterity, and steadfast devotion to her family all at once – this is the truly wonderful KIM1.
